Easy Mashed Potatoes

Description

This recipe is perfect if you love some good, old fashioned
mashed potatoes. Easy to make, simple, and delicious. These
potatoes are great for a side dish and super quick as well.
Of course if you wanted to you could use less milk and/or
more butter to make the recipe even more perfect for your
own taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds baking potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil
  2. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm
    (about 15 minutes) and drain
  3. In a small saucepan heat butter and milk over
    low heat until butter is melted
  4. Using a potato masher, slowling blend milk mixture
    into potatoes until smooth and creamy
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste
