Chicken and Rice

Description

This "no peek" chicken and rice will have your
house smelling amazing! Super easy meal that
works well for anyone, including picky eaters.
There are a lot of substitutes that you can
make that will still make it taste that great.

Ingredients

  • 1 box Uncle Ben's Long Grain Wild Rice (original recipe)
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can cream of celery soup
  • 1 can water (can add another can for more moistured rice)
  • 3-5 chicken breasts or tenders

Steps

  1. Grease a 9 x 13 pan
  2. Mix the box of rice, cans of soup, and can(s) of water
  3. Arrange the raw chicken on top of the rice mixture
  4. Cover and seal with foil
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 2 1/2 hours and "Don't Peek!"
