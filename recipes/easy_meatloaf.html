Easy Meatloaf

Description

Very easy recipe with no fails possible. This recipe
makes for a very quick meal with an amazing taste.
Endless possbilities to make the recipe perfect for
you and your family. If you don't feel too keen on
the sauce the recipe wants you to make, worcestershire
sauce makes it even more delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped (frozen works too)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs (or instant oatmeal)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • 1/3 cup ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine beef, egg, onion, milk, and bread crumbs into large bowl
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Place in a light greased 9 x 5 loaf pan
  5. In a seperate bowl, combine brown sugar, mustard and ketchup
  6. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
